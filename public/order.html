Payment Options

Checkboxes = which options the selected package offers. Rates and factors apply to every package.

Cash
$500 deposit at signing · 50% at permit · 40% at install scheduling · 10% at inspection.
Financed (loan)

Each term shows as its own card, e.g. “$322.76/mo · 30.5 years”.

Lease (LightReach)

25-year term, $0 down, first payment after PTO. Take the lender quote for this job ÷ order total × 10,000 to get the rate.

Prepaid lease

Shown to the customer as: Project Total − discount = Net Cost.

Prepaid lease — financed

Applied to the net (discounted) prepaid amount. Each term shows the customer its own monthly price — e.g. 10 yr @ 13.40 on a $19,518 net = $261.54/mo.
